About Me

I'm a vibrant individual whose creativity knows no bounds, with a penchant for music, dance, poetry, and art. Hailing from the historic town of Devakottai in Sivagangai district, Tamil Nadu, and currently residing in the bustling city of Coimbatore, my life is infused with a rich tapestry of culture and experiences. Alongside my artistic pursuits, I share my home with a beloved canine companion, embodying both warmth and companionship.

My eclectic tastes span across various forms of entertainment, from the melodies of diverse music genres to the captivating narratives of feel-good and romantic films, and the rich tapestry of Tamil history found within the pages of books. An extroverted spirit, I thrive in social settings, relishing the opportunity to connect with others and explore new adventures.

While I cherish moments spent outdoors, engaging in activities that invigorate both body and soul, my aspirations extend to professional endeavors, with a focus on excelling in a reputable company. Yet, amidst the hustle and bustle of life, my heart yearns for the exploration of historical landmarks, fueling my wanderlust and desire for cultural enrichment.
